## Short description (80 char max)

## Full description (4000 char max)
Sheaf is a fast, private PDF toolkit for Android. Everything happens on your device —
no accounts, no ads, no tracking, and your documents never leave your phone.

READ
• Smooth page rendering with pinch-to-zoom
• Remembers where you left off in every document
• Light, Dark, Sepia, and System reading themes
• Full-text search and table-of-contents navigation
• Print and share any PDF

ANNOTATE & SIGN
• Draw freehand ink and highlight passages
• Drop sticky notes anywhere on a page
• Capture your signature once and stamp it on any document
• See all your annotations in one list and jump straight to them

FILL FORMS
• Fill interactive PDF (AcroForm) fields right on the page
• Save and share the completed document

ORGANISE PAGES
• Rotate, delete, and reorder pages
• Save the result as a new document

SCAN (on-device)
• Capture multi-page documents with automatic edge detection and cropping
• Straight to a clean PDF, added to your library

SHEAF PRO — a one-time purchase, no subscription
• OCR: turn scanned images into searchable text
• Reduce file size by re-compressing images
• Password-protect a PDF with AES encryption

PRIVACY FIRST
Sheaf processes your files locally. Scanning and OCR run on-device using Google ML Kit,
and the only network access is for Google Play (purchases). We don't collect your data.

Made for people who just want their PDFs to work — quickly, cleanly, and privately.
