Sheaf — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

Sheaf ("the app") is a PDF reader and toolkit for Android. Sheaf is built to keep your documents on your device. It has no user accounts, shows no ads, and contains no third-party analytics or tracking.

What the app accesses

  • Your files: only files you open or import; they remain on your device. Edited, filled, compressed, protected, or scanned copies are stored privately or shared only when you choose.
  • Camera (scanning): uses the Google Play services ML Kit Document Scanner, which runs in Google's own UI and processes images on-device. Sheaf does not require the camera permission itself.
  • OCR: performed on-device with Google ML Kit; recognized text becomes a searchable layer in your PDF and is not sent to us.
  • Purchases (Sheaf Pro): handled by Google Play Billing. Sheaf stores only a local "Pro unlocked" flag and never receives your payment details.
  • Network: used only for Google Play (purchases) and Google Play services components. Sheaf does not send your documents over the network.

Data we collect

None. Sheaf does not collect, store, or share personal data on our own servers.

Third-party services

Google Play services / ML Kit (scanning, on-device OCR) and Google Play Billing (purchases), each governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Children

Sheaf is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.
