# Sheaf — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 5 July 2026_

Sheaf ("the app") is a PDF reader and toolkit for Android. This policy explains what the app does and
does not do with your information. **Sheaf is built to keep your documents on your device.**

## Summary
- Sheaf has **no user accounts**, shows **no ads**, and contains **no third-party analytics or tracking**.
- Your PDFs and their contents are processed **locally on your device**. Sheaf does not upload your
  documents to any server operated by us.

## What the app accesses
- **Your files:** Only files you explicitly open or import. They remain on your device. Edited, filled,
  compressed, protected, or scanned copies are written to the app's private storage or shared only when
  you choose to share them.
- **Camera (document scanning):** Scanning uses the Google Play services ML Kit Document Scanner, which
  runs in Google's own interface. Captured images are processed **on-device** to produce a PDF. Sheaf does
  not require the camera permission itself and does not transmit captured images to us.
- **Text recognition (OCR):** Performed **on-device** with Google's ML Kit. Recognized text is written
  into your PDF as a searchable layer and is not sent to us.
- **Purchases (Sheaf Pro):** In-app purchases are handled by **Google Play Billing**. Google processes the
  transaction; Sheaf only stores a local flag indicating whether Pro is unlocked. We do not receive or
  store your payment details.
- **Network:** The app uses the internet only to communicate with Google Play for purchases and to allow
  Google Play services components (scanner/OCR models) to function. Sheaf does not send your documents
  over the network.

## Data we collect
None. Sheaf does not collect, store, or share personal data on our own servers.

## Third-party services
- **Google Play services / ML Kit** (document scanning, on-device OCR) — see Google's Privacy Policy.
- **Google Play Billing** (purchases) — see Google's Privacy Policy.

## Children
Sheaf is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

We may update this policy; the "Last updated" date will change accordingly.
